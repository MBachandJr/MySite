Michael Bachand Jr.

Computer Science - Aviation - Leadership

My name is Michael Bachand Jr. I'm a student at the CREC Academy of Aerospace and Engineering in Windsor Connecticut, a BSA Senior Patrol Leader, and a Cadet First Lieutenant in the Civil Air Patrol where I serve as the Cadet Commander of the 186th Composite Squadron located in Plainville Connecticut, as the Vice Chair of the Connecticut Wing Cadet Advisory Council, and as the Vice Chair for the North East Region Strategic Communications Coordination Commitee.

In school, I am currently working on the planning of a fully enclosed cockpit flight simulator that will eventually be used by the school to introduce students to the feeling of being inside of - and flying - an airplane. I will be preforming this project as an independent study and will hopefully have the simulator finished and working by January of 2018.
